Manifesto

A GLOBAL
CHALLENGE

In 2050, there will be
9 billion people on Earth.

Not only will there be increased demand for food, but we will be facing the major challenges of diminishing resources and environmental degradation.

Against this backdrop, ALGAMA is committed to creating and producing alternative foods, for the benefit of all.

A SOLUTION
WITH HUGE POTENTIAL

ALGAMA exploits the exceptional nutritional qualities of microalgae.

Thanks to their unique nutritional value, microalgae are used for many innovative solutions in the fields of green energy and chemicals, cosmetics, nutrition and health. When it comes to food and nutrition, spirulina and chlorella are the ‘stars’ because of their exceptional nutritional properties.
These microalgae are likely to play a key role in global food supply, thanks to their unique protein content and the low environmental impact of the production process.

business expertise:

RESEARCH &
DEVELOPMENT

ALGAMA’s core activity is the creation of ingredients and innovative products derived from the properties of microalgae. The R&D department’s first task is to identify and select microalgae with unique attributes so that they can be used to develop consumer products.

PRODUCT
INNOVATION

Our R&D and marketing teams and culinary consultants work in close partnership from the earliest stages of product design and creation.

The team’s objective is to develop and deliver innovative and tasty food products with high market potential while overcoming the constraints and technological barriers involved in extracting the nutrients contained in microalgae for the food sector.

Our history:

2013

Founding of the company

Winners of numerous innovation contests, Alvyn, Gaëtan and Mathieu decide to set up their company, Algama. Creation of Springwave, Algama's first product, developed with the support of strategy, technical and technology experts.

2014

Year of achievement

Algama wins several prizes, both in France and internationally. Springwave wins two prizes at the SIAL, in a field of 1200 competing products, including Best Non-alcoholic Beverage. Algama's R&D teams relocate to Agoranov to develop a variety of new products.

2015

Marketing of

Algama continues its expansion with major strategic industrial alliances: global partnership with the Roquette Frères Group, joint stand at Expo Milano 2015. Algama also sets up its new food lab at Genopole, Evry, the first biocluster in France. In late 2015, presentation of a new Algama product, planned to be marketed in the first quarter of 2016.

2016

International
expansion

Setting up of an international office and establishment of GB/US operations (Springwave and Algama). Public launch of a second Algama product in France.

2017

International structure & accelerated R&D

Structuring of international operations; recruitment of local teams. Accelerated R&D activities with the launching of three new products.
